Getting started

Please search the student name or student id number to begin reviewing the application.

The review process consists of 3 main categories. Each of which are dependent on the previous category.

For example, an applicant must meet the minumum academic requirements before the assigned reviewers can begin grading them on their non-academic requirements. Similarly, an applicant can not begin the interview process without meeting the minumum requirements of the non-academic category.
