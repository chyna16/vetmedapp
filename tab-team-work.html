What the student sees on the door

A discussion of how the student could set the group up to increase participation without being confrontational may include delegating specific tasks to specific members…but what if that doesn’t work? How well can the candidate solve this problem?

Probing Questions

You have been assigned to work with 3 of your classmates in a team exercise where you must work collaboratively to finish an assignment for a grade. One of your classmates (the loud one) tends to talk over and monopolizes the conversation while another (the laid back one) is not contributing at all and actually seems to be making excuses to not show up for group meetings.

Issues to consider:

  1. Could you set up the group so that everyone makes contributions without being confrontational?

  2. Tell me about a similar situation you have experienced and how you handled it?

Followup questions

  1. The loud student becomes belligerent and angry. How would you handle that?
